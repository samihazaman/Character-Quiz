House Descriptions

Gryffindor

Gryffindor values bravery, daring, nerve, and chivalry. Its emblematic animal is the lion and its colors are scarlet and gold. The founder of the house is Godric Gryffindor.

Hufflepuff

Hufflepuff values hard work, dedication, patience, loyalty, and fair play. Its emblematic animal is the badger, and yellow and black are its colors. The founder of the house is Helga Hufflepuff.

Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw values intelligence, knowledge, and wit. Its emblematic animal is the eagle and its colors are blue and bronze (or blue and silver, according to the films). The founder of the house is Rowena Ravenclaw.

Slytherin

Slytherin values ambition, cunning, leadership, and resourcefulness. Its emblematic animal is the serpent and its colors are green and silver. The founder of the house is Salazar Slytherin.
